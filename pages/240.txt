[Devanagari unclear: verses on individual meat properties]

Meat of the goat is not too cold, too heavy and too unctuous does not vitiate
doṣas, because of similarity with dhātus it promotes the bulk of body and does
not block the channels. Mutton is sweet, cold, heavy and bulk-promoting. Cow and
sheep do not come decidedly in any of the above eight groups because of their meat
habitat. [61-62]
