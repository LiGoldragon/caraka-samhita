एकविंशोऽध्यायः
CHAPTER XXI

अथातोऽष्टौनिन्दितीयमध्यायं व्याख्यास्यामः ॥१॥
Now (I) shall expound the chapter on the eight despicables etc. [1]

इति ह स्वाह भगवानात्रेयः ॥१॥
As propounded by Lord Atreya. [2]

इह खलु शरीरिमधुरकृत्याहौ पुरुषा निन्दिता भवन्ति; तद्यथा–अतिदीर्घश्च, अतिह्रस्वश्च, अतिलोमश च, अतिरोमकृश्च, अतिकृष्णश्च, अतिगौरश्च, अतिस्थूलश्च, अतिकृशश्च ॥३॥
In context of body, the eight persons are despicable such as–over-tall, over-short, over-hairy, hairless, over-block, over-fair, over-obese, over-lean. [3]

अत्रास्थूलकृशयोर्भूय एवापरे निन्दितविशेषा भवन्ति। अतिस्थूलस्य तावदष्टौ दोषाः प्रयोपरोधः कुत्सितव्यायामता दीर्घं दौर्बल्यं मेदोवेदनामेदः; कुत्सितस्राविणां पुनरतियोगाहित्वाच्छास्त्रनिर्दोषाणां तद्यथ–स्नेहगुरुशीतनिद्राप्रसङ्गाच्छास्त्रस्वाभाव्यमेद एव वर्धते; मेदसः स्नेहसंसर्गाभिधानदुभयापघाताद्भयाद्धातुसाम्यावस्थानादर्शनाच्च स्वेदातिरेकः; मेदोऽतिस्नेहगुरुत्वादव्यायामतोलेपादग्निर्हीयते; शुक्राल्पतया मैथुनासामर्थ्यम्; तमाद्वाय्वाधिक्याद्गुदामाशयस्वेदाधिक्याद्बहुशोऽशनपिपासे ॥४॥

Amongst them, the over-obese and the over-lean have other despicable features too. The over-obese has eight defects–shortening of life-span, hampered movement, difficulty in sexual intercourse, debility, foul smell, over-sweating, too much hunger and excessive thirst. Over-obesity is caused by over-saturation, intake of heavy, sweet, cold and fatty diet, indulgence in day-sleeping and exhilaration, lack of mental work and genetic defect. There is excess of fat in him and further only fat is accumulated and not the other dhātus so, thus the life-span is shortened; because by laxity, softness and heaviness of fat there is hampering in movement; due to non-abundance of semen and the passage having been covered with fat, there is difficulty in sexual intercourse; due to disequilibrium of dhātus there is debility; foul smell is due to defect and nature of fat and also sweating, due to association of medas with kapha, its oozing nature, abundance, heaviness and intolerance to physical exercise there is over-sweating; because of intensified agni (digestion) and abundance of vāyu in belly there is excessive hunger and thirst. [4]

भवति चात्र–
मेदसऽऽदुत्तमगुणात्तव्याधेः कोष्ठे विशेषतः। चरन् संयुक्तयस्थपिभागहारे शोचनस्थपि ॥५॥
तस्मान् स शीघ्रं जरयत्याशु सातिवाहिनी विकारांश्च बहूंश्च घोरान् काश्किञ्चित्स्थलित्क्षणात् ॥६॥
