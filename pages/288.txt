[Devanagari unclear: verses on those without authority]

Those who are not authority in their own treatise often condemn others taking
resort to ignoble views, lack of time, illness, arrogance and harshness. One should
keep away from these scripture-defiles who simulate the noose of death and should
be devoted to the good physicians who are full of calmness, knowledge and under-
standing. [81-83]

[Devanagari unclear: verses on entire happiness]

Entire misery relating to both (mind and body) depends on ignorance, the entire
happiness resides in pure knowledge. This treatise with wide scope is not illuminat-
ing to the ignorant ones as the sun to the blind. [84-85]
