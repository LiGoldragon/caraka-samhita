Life-span has its non-measure in case of conflict between strong and weak na-
tures of body and the contradicting factors of place, time, etc. Such non-measure
becomes manifest in normal cases due to the same reasons. (Some scholars opine
that)–"Life-span is conditioned by daiva (the destiny in the form of effects of pre-
vious deeds) only, the views of those scholars are not correct because the variations
in life-span are observed even with similar deeds and the cessation of life-span is
also seen due to non-deeds because if the life-span be only conditioned by 'daiva'
the variations in length of life-span will not be possible because of similarity of
deeds. Why and to whom Āyurveda? The reply is to those who want a long life-
span endowed with all the desired things and Āyurveda is to make this objective
fulfilled. [25-26]
