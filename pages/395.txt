[Devanagari unclear: long verse on discussion]

Hereafter (I) shall describe the method of discussion. A physician should discuss
with a physician. Discussion with specialists promotes the pursuit and advancement
of knowledge, provides dexterity, improves power of speaking, illumines fame,
removes doubt in scriptures, if any, by repeating the topics, and creates confidence
in case there is no doubt, brings forth some new ideas hitherto unknown, by reason
is that whatever secret ideas are gradually delivered by the teacher pleased over
the devoted disciple, the same are expressed by him in enthusiasm during discussion
in order to gain victory. Hence the experts recommend the discussion with the
specialists. [15]

[Devanagari unclear: long verse on two types of discussion]

Discussion with specialists is of two types–friendly discussion and ho tile dis-
cussion. [16]
