488                           CARAKA-SAṂHITĀ                             [CH

[Devanagari unclear: verse on Śārīra section]

This section is called as 'Śārīra' (pertaining to human body) because in this the
entire body is studied in all aspects and also for knowing the godly and manly excel-
lence of the same. [68-69]

इत्यग्निवेशकृते तन्त्रे चरकप्रतिसंस्कृते शारीरस्थाने जातिसूत्रीयं
शारीरं नामाष्टमोऽध्यायः ॥८॥

Thus ends the eighth chapter on the principles of procreation in
Śārīrasthāna in the treatise composed by Agniveśa
and redacted by Caraka. (8)

शारीरस्थानं संपूर्णम् ।

Here ends the section on the study of human body.
