इति वादमार्गपदानि यथोद्देशमभिनिर्दिष्टानि भवन्ति ॥६६॥

Thus the terms used in course of debate are explained as proposed. [66]

[Devanagari unclear: long verse on debate among physicians]

The debate among physicians should be held only on (topics of) Āyurveda
and not elsewhere. Here the statements and counter-statements in details and also all
the arguments have been mentioned in the entire text. After keeping them in mind
one should speak something or not anything which is irrelevant, unscriptural, unexamined,
inappropriate, confused or unpervasive. He should speak everything with reasoning.
All the conflicts of debate, if endowed with reasoning, are devoid of malice and pro-
mote the (cause) of medicine due to its property of enhancing the excellence of intel-
lect, because undamaged intellect leads to success in all actions. [67]

[Devanagari unclear: long verse on physician acting after knowing]

Now (I) shall explain some entities for the knowledge of physicians because the
wise commend initiation of all actions with prior knowledge. If after knowing well
kāraṇa, karaṇa, kāryayoni, kārya, kāryaphala, anubandha, deśa, kāla, pravṛtti and
upāya one proceeds for some action, he obtains the desired fruit and subsequent
benefit in that without any great effort. [68]

[Devanagari unclear: verse on kāraṇa]

Kāraṇa (doer) is that who does a thing, he is the cause and agent. [69]

[Devanagari unclear: verse on karaṇa]

Karaṇa (instrument) is that which serves as equipment for the doer making effort
for performing the action. [70]

[Devanagari unclear: verse on Kāryayoni]

Kāryayoni (original source) is that which is converted into action after transfor-
mation. [71]
