[Devanagari unclear: verse on intellect]

Wrong understanding by the intellect and wrong actions (accordingly) should
be known as intellectual error which is committed by mind. [109]

[Devanagari unclear: long verse on cause of disorders]

Genesis of disorders due to time factor is already said in the chapter on enumera-
tion of disorders and also accumulation, aggravation and pacification of pitta etc. The
seasons ending with the rainy one having characters as perverted, excessive or under-
mined which are cause of disorders, timely and untimely condition of food having
been digested, ingested and being digested, three divisions of the day–forenoon, mid-
day and afternoon, similar ones of the night–these disorders related to these times are
known as caused by the time factor. [110-112]

[Devanagari unclear: verse on diseases by time]

(For instance) various forms of malarial fever such as quotidian, severe tertian
tertian and quartan, arise in their respective times because they attain strength in those
particular times. [113]

[Devanagari unclear: verse on therapy]

These and other various time-caused diseases should be treated before they arise
with the due consideration of their strength and time. [114]

[Devanagari unclear: long verse on counteracting]

By the succession of time, natural disorders caused by old age and approaching
death occur because nature can not be counteracted. [115]

[Devanagari unclear: verse on daiva]

The deed of the past life known as 'daiva' (fate) is also observed as the cause of
diseases in (particular) time. [116]

[Devanagari unclear: verse on phala]

There is no major action the fruit of which is not enjoyed. The diseases caused
by the deeds (of past life) neutralize the therapeutic measures and subside only on
destruction of the deeds. [117]
