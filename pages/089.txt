48                            CARAKA-SAṂHITĀ                             [CH.

बस्तिमेहन्योः; शूलं मूत्रकृच्छ्र शिरोरुजा । विनामो वंक्षणानाहाः: स्याल्लिङ्गं मूत्रनिग्रहे ॥६॥
स्वेदावगाहनाभ्यञ्ज सर्पिःपानार्ववगाहनम् । मूत्रे प्रतिहते कुर्याद्विधिनाश्वनिषेडितम् च ॥७॥

By suppression of (the urge for) urination, the symptoms caused are pain in
urinary bladder and passage, dysuria, headache, bending, stiffness in groins. In case
of obstructed urine, fomentation, tub-bath, massage, intake of ghee, fomentation and
be prescribed and also the three types of Basti (enema) and catheterisation. [6-7]

पक्रशयोऽपि:शूलं वातौला्रुवर्तनम् । पिथिकाकोडेरणानामी पुरीषे स्वधिताहरि ॥८॥
स्वेदाभ्यङ्गावगाहाश्च वर्त्यो बस्तिकर्म च । हितं प्रतिहते वर्चसुचुनिग्रहणानि च ॥९॥

Suppression of (the urge) of defaecation causes colic pain, headache, retention
of flatus and faeces, cramps in calf muscles and flatulence. In retention of faeces,
fomentation, massage, tub-bath, suppositories, and enema are prescribed and also the
food and drinks which are obstruction-removing. [8-9]

मेढ़े वृषणयो: शूलमङ्गमर्दो हृदि व्यथा । भवेर्मूत्रतिग्रहे श्रुक्रे विरब्धं मुत्रमेव च ॥१०॥
तत्राभ्यङ्गोऽगाहश्च मदिरा षष्टिकान्नमा । शालिर्दुष्षमविकाकुकूकुटं मांसानि च भक्षयेत् ॥११॥

In retention of semen, the symptoms caused are pain in penis and scrotum,
body-ache, pain in cardiac region and also obstruction in urine. In that case, massage,
tub-bath, wines, chicken, rice, milk, non-unctuous enema and sexual intercourse are
prescribed. [10-11]

सङ्गौ विण्मूत्रवातानामाध्मानं वेदना क्लमः: । जठरे वातजाह्यायी रोगाः; स्युर्वातनिग्रहे ॥१२॥
स्नेहेपोदीर्ताह्यस्तानं सर्वचोभोजनानि च । पानान्नैः बतजान् रोगान् रक्षियेत् वातनिग्रहे ॥१३॥

Suppression of flatus causes retention of faeces, urine and flatus, flatulence,
pain, exhaustion and other diorders in abdomen due to vāta. There, oleation, fomenta-
tion, suppositories, food and drinks and other carminative measures are
recommended. [12-13]

कण्डूकोटास्त्विमरुदारोर्मगमावचयस्थः । कुष्ठकुसलान्तलयर्ल्सक्त्यीशिष्ठमिलाद्र तथा ॥१४॥
छदगाः प्रकृप्तविं धूमो लङ्घनैयधाक्कणम् । आकाष्यास्नात्यान्म्यालायाः वित्त्नसताप्रं स्रग्थूल गुन्नाहु ॥१५॥

By suppression of vomiting, the diseases caused are itching, urticarial rashes,
anorexia, blackish spots on face, swelling, anaemia, fever, skin diseases, nausea and
erysipelas. In that case, induction of vomiting after feeding, smoking, fasting (or light
diet), blood-letting, rough diet, physical exercises and purgation are prescribed. [14-15]

मन्यास्तम्भः: शिरःशूलमर्दितं चार्द्ध्भेदकम् । इन्द्रियाणां च दौर्बल्यं स्याद्धुषुप्रा्यहात् ॥१६॥
तत्रोगलेश्यावन्व्यङ्गाश्च स्नेहो वेदो मृजुभोजनम् ।

Suppression of sneezing or sneezing rough diet causes stiffness of sideneck, headache,
paralysis, migraine and weakness of sense organs. There, massage in head-neck
region, fomentation, smoking, nasal drops, vāta-pacifying diet and post-meal intake
of ghee are prescribed. [16-17]
