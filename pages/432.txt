[Devanagari unclear: long verse on unctuous enema]

Hereafter (I) shall mention the drugs used for unctuous enema. 'anuvāsana' is
of two types–vegetable products and animal products. The vegetable products are
either taila (oil) derived for tila–sesamum seeds or ataila (other than the above),
but both of them are described here as 'taila' because of overall predominance of
tila oil. Animal products are fat, marrow and ghee. Out of the oil, fat, marrow
and ghee, excellence of applicability as unctuous enema in disorders of vāta and
kapha, is determined in regressive order while that is determined in progressive
order in paittika disorders, or all are applicable in all disorders according to particular
processing. [150]
