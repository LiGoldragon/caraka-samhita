[Devanagari unclear: long verse on kuṣṭha prodroma]

These are their prodromal symptoms such as–loss of or excessive perspiration,
roughness or excessive smoothness, abnormal colour, itching, piercing pain, numb-
ness, general burning sensation, tingling sensation, horripilation, coarseness,
heating, smearing in body orifices, excessive pain in suppurated, burnt, bitten,
broken, injured and dislocated parts, suppuration and non-healing of even small
wounds. [7]

[Devanagari unclear: long verse on kuṣṭha kapālakuṣṭha]

After this kuṣṭhas manifest. Their pain, colour, symptoms, effect and name are
known as follows:– rough, reddish, dark and uneven spread, having coarse edges,
with elevated thin periphery, extremely numbed, covered with erect hairs, having
excessive piercing pain and little itching, burning, pus and lymph, with fast
development, causation and decay, infested with organisms, and having colour like
blackish red earthen piece is known as kapālakuṣṭha. (1)

[Devanagari unclear: verse on udumbarakuṣṭha]

blood and lymph; having itching, discharge, sloughing, heat and suppuration, with
fast development, causation and decay, associated with temperature and discomfort,
having colour like that of the udumbara fruits is known as audumbara kuṣṭha. (2)
