सैन्यं लवणानां, जीवन्तिशाकः शाकानां, इणेण मृगमांसानां, इणाः पक्षिणां, गोधा विलेशयमानां, रोहिती मत्स्यानां,
गर्थं सर्पिः पार्षिणां, गोक्षीरं क्षीरणां, तिलतैलं व्याव्यायानामां लेहानां, वराशहसन आणुपूर्मांसानां, शुक्तलोकेशस
मांसभसानां, पार्क्षेत्रमस जंत्वाभिरक्षपूर्वानां, कुक्कुटस्य विचिरकराकूनिपूर्वसानां, अजनैतिः शलाञ्जनैश्च, भूशुरेरे
कन्दानां, मृहीका फलानां, शर्करोष्कुकिराणाम्, इति प्रकृतिये हितमाहारमाहारविकारणां याश्यानि व्याख्यानि
व्याख्यावानि भवन्ति ११३६११

Such as red śāli rice is the best wholesome among the awned cereals, green
gram among the legumes (pulses), rain water among the various types of water, rock
salt among salts, Jīvantī among the pot-herbs, meat of antelope among the animal
meats, common quail among the birdmeal, iguana among the meat of animals living
in holes, rohita among fish, cow-ghee among the ghees, cow-milk among the milks,
sesamum oil among the vegetable oils, lard among the fats of marshy animals, fat of
culukī among the fish fats, fat of pākahansa (swan) among the fats of aquatic animals,
fat of cock among the fats of gallinaceous birds, fat of goat among the fats of plant-
eating animals, ginger among the tubers, grapes among fruits, sugar among the
products of sugarcane. Thus the substances of diet which are naturally wholesome
are explained according to importance. [38]
