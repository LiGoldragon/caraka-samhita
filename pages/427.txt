of sweet group should be taken and after cutting and breaking them into small
pieces and washing with water properly should be placed in a vessel. Adding to it
milk diluted with half water should be cooked stirring constantly with a ladle. When
the major portion of water is absorbed, drugs are extracted and milk is not charred
the vessel should be brought down and the milk should be taken out of it and is
filtered well. This lukewarm milk added with ghee, oil, fat, marrow, salt and phāṇita
should be used properly for enema by expert in the disorders of vāta. In paittika
disorders, the same should be given as cold and combined with honey and ghee.
Thus ends the sweet group. [139]
