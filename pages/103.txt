नवमोऽध्यायः
CHAPTER IX

अधाः: झुड्डाकचतुष्पादमध्यायं व्याख्यास्यामः ।।१।।

Now (I) shall expound the chapter on small quadruped. (1)

इति ह स्माह पराशरावेशः ।।२।।

As propounded by Lord Ātreya [2].

चिकित्सद्वयोपध्यास्था रोगी पाथुष्ठदम् । गुणवत् कारणं श्रेयं विकारव्युपशान्तये ।।३।।

Physician, drug, attendant and patient, this is the quadruped which, if endowed
with qualities, leads to alleviation of disorders. [3]

विकारो धातुवैषम्यं, साम्यं प्रकृतिरुच्यते । सुखमस्रोगमारोग्यं, विकारो दुःखमेव च ।।४।।

Disorder (disorder) is disequilibrium of dhātus and their equilibrium is
normalcy (health). Health is known as happiness while disorder is unhappiness. [4]

चतुर्णां चिकयगतीनां शस्तानां धातुवैकृते । प्रयोक्तिर्गुतेषामार्थां चिकित्सेत्याभिधीयते ।।५।।

Employment of all the excellent four–physician etc.–in case of disorder of dhātus
with the object of (re-establishing) their equilibrium is said as therapeutics. [5]

श्रुते र्यर्पवातवां बहुशो दृष्टकर्मता । दाक्ष्यं शौचमिति श्रेयं वैद्यो गुणचतुष्टयम् ।।६।।

Thoroughness in theoretical knowledge, extensive practical experience, dexterity
and cleanliness–this is the quadruple of qualities of a physician. [6]
