[Devanagari unclear: verse on śarīradhātus]

These factors lead to the growth of the body in its entirety such as–obsevance of
time, natural process, excellence of diet and absence of damage. [12]

[Devanagari unclear: long verse on factors of strength]

These factors lead to the increase of strength such as–birth in a place having
strong persons, that in a time conducive for strength, favourable time, excellence of
seed and soil, excellence of diet, body, suitability and psyche; natural mechanism,
youth, physical exercise and cheerfulness. [13]

[Devanagari unclear: verse on factors of transformation]

These are the factors leading to transformation of food such as–heat, vāyu, mois-
ture, unctuousness, time and balanced use. [14]

[Devanagari unclear: verse on functions of these factors]

These are the functions of the factors like heat etc. which cause transformation
of food such as–heat digests, vāyu absorbs, moisture produces looseness, unction
generates softness, time brings sufficiency, balanced use brings about the equilibrium
of dhātus which are the products of food. [15]
