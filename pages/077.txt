36                            CARAKA-SAṂHITĀ                             [CH.

स्नैहिकं धूमपाने दोषे वायुः; पित्तानुगो यदि । शीतं तु रक्ष्यिते स्याल्लेप्नोपिते विश्वकणम् ।।४०।।

In such cases, intake of ghee, snuff, collyrium and saturating is desirable. In
case of complications due to smoking, these should be the unctuous if vāyu follows pitta,
cold in raktapitta and roughening in kapha-pitta. [39]

धूमयोग: सकृत्वर्थ्या धूमं घेषो विनिर्दिष्टा ।
न रक्षौ न विषेणात्ते न स्नोजो वा गर्भिणीम् ।।४१।।
न रक्खे दुखितं च हद्धं न च मद्ये नगो या विषै च ग्रसस्तनम् ।।४२।।
न मुच्छार्चप्रभृत्यायु न बाधों नावि न क्षते । न मधक्ति मांद्यदेव च । लेहिनः परखच्छै: कीर्ति मधोऽपि न राउिडकानु भाति ।
न शङ्खकेच न रौहिण्यां न मदात्यये च मेहे ।। पशु धूमकाले्षु नोहाद् चिन्ना साबि न तरि ।।४५।।
रोगास्तस्य प्रवर्तन्ते दारुणा धूमविभ्रमात् ।

Now I shall say about person smoking is contra-indicated. One should
not smoke after purgation, enema and bleeding, one afflicted with poison, anxiety,
pregnant, in case of fatigue, narcosis, āma, pitta, vigil, fainting giddiness, thirst,
emaciation, injury; after taking wine, milk, fatty substances and honey, food with
curd; in conditions of roughness, anger, dryness of palate, defects of vision, head
injury, Śaṅkhaka, rohinī, prameha and alcoholism. One who smoke in these condi-
tions and untimely due to carelessness, the disorders aggravate severly due to com-
plication of smoking. [41-45]

धूमयोगः: पिबेद्योने शिरोप्राणागसंश्रया । १४६।।
प्राणेनायम्य मूर्ख्य सुखं प्राणयो वषेत् । आत्स्येन धूमकवलमन्तिः पिबन् धायेंन योजभेत् ।।४७।।
प्रतिलोमो गतो द्राक्ष्या धूमो हिर्स्यादि चक्षुषी ।

One fit for smoking should smoke through nostrils in disorders located in head,
nose and eyes, and through mouth in those of throat. While smoking through nostrils,
one should emit through mouth but while smoking through mouth, one should not
emit through nostrils because the smoke traversing reversely damages the eyes imme-
diately. [46-47]

ऋज्वज्जवहृतस्वेताः: सुपविष्टोसर्गप्रयुक्तः ।।४८।।
पिबेन्निष्ट पिधायैका नासया धूमयात्मवान् ।

One should smoke through one nostril closing the other one three rounds, thrice
in each round, cautiously while sitting comfortably with straight body and eyes and
concentrated mind. [48]

चतुर्विंशतिकं नैव स्त्राङ्गुलीभिर्विंसने ।।४९।।
द्वाविंशत्युलं स्नेहे प्रयोगेऽष्ठद्शात्मिते ।

The smoking pipe should be, by own fingers, of twenty four fingers length
in evacuation, of thirty two fingers in snuff and of eighteen fingers in routine
smoking. [49]

ऋजु किकोचिकारहितं कोलास्थ्यमधुनिर्गतम् ।।५०।।
वस्तिनेत्रसमद्मव्यं धूमनेत्रं प्रशस्यते ।
