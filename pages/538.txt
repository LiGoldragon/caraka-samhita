[Devanagari unclear: verse on water]

If in a clean water, stable or flowing, and without having a net one sees net, he
is deprived of life. [9]

[Devanagari unclear: long verse on visualising ghosts]

While awake if one visualises ghosts and various demons or some other surpris-
ing thing, he is unable to live. [10]

[Devanagari unclear: long verse on fire colour]

One who sees the normal-coloured fire as blue, lustreless, black or white goes to
the seventh night (death). [11]

[Devanagari unclear: long verse on visualising clouds]

If one sees the rays of light without clouds, or clouds or lightening when there
are no clouds, he attains death. [12]

[Devanagari unclear: long verse on signs of moon]

Whether diseased or not if one visualises solar or lunar eclipse in their absence,
it indicates his death.

If one sees the sun in night, or the moon in day or smoke arisen in absence of fire
or lustreless fire in night, he is about to die.

When one is going to give up his existence, he sees brilliant objects as devoid
of brilliance, or non-brilliant objects as brillant ones or other objects devoid of
their normal characters. The persons, at the end of life, see the objects as deformed,
of different colour, in different number without an apperent cause. If one sees
the non-visible objects or does not see the visible one–both types of persons die
soon. [13–18]
