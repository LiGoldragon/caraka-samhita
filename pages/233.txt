These are the measures, which are used to counteract the above and other disor-

भवन्ति चात्र–

Here are (the verses)–

Purgation, emesis, pacification or prior use of wholesome substance alleviates
the disorders caused by antagonistic food. The antagonism becomes inert due to suit-
ability, small quantity, strong digestive power, in young age and persons having uc-
tion, physical exercise, and strength. [105-106]

तत्र श्लोकाः–

[Devanagari unclear: summary verses]

Here are the (summing up) verses–

Different views of the sages regarding rasa, drugs according to properties and
actions, number of drugs according to rasa, argument in the number (six) of rasa,
definition of rasa and anurasa; and of parādi guṇas, division of five bhūtas in six
rasas, preponderance of properties in up-moving and down-moving drugs, variations
in combination of rasas, characters and exceptions in properties and actions of drugs
(rasa), three degrees of rasa according to guṇa etc. definition of vipāka and prabhāva,
decision about the number of vīrya, characters of six rasas, the antagonism, the dis-
eases produced by it alongwith their treatment–all this has been said by the sage in
the chapter of Ātreya, Bhadrakāpya etc. [107-113]

इत्यग्निवेशकृते तन्त्रे चरकप्रतिसंस्कृते श्लोकस्थाने आत्रेयभद्रकाप्यीयो
नाम षड्विंशोऽध्यायः ॥२६॥

Thus ends the twenty sixth chapter on Ātreya Bhadrakāpya etc. in
Ślokasthāna in the treatise composed by Agniveśa and
redacted by Caraka. (26)
