# Śārīrasthāna — Section on the Study of Human Body

*Source pages 435-526 of Sharma 2014, Vol. I.*

The Śārīrasthāna ("section on the body") treats the metaphysics and embryology of the *puruṣa* — the constituents of the personal self, the descent of consciousness into the womb, the month-by-month development of the foetus, the macrocosm-microcosm correspondence, the enumeration of body parts, and the long *jātisūtrīya* on procreation, parturition, post-partum care, naming, the wet-nurse, breast-milk pathology, the nursery, and child-rearing. It contains 8 chapters.

## Chapters

1. [Katidhāpuruṣīya](01-katidha-purushiya.md) — on the types of personal self (pp. 435-449)
2. [Atulyagotrīya](02-atulyagotriya.md) — on dissimilar clan (pp. 450-456)
3. [Khuḍḍākagarbhāvakrānti-śārīra](03-khuddaka-garbhavakranti.md) — on the descent of embryo, minor (pp. 456-465)
4. [Mahatīgarbhāvakrānti-śārīra](04-mahati-garbhavakranti.md) — on the descent of embryo, major (pp. 466-477)
5. [Puruṣavicaya-śārīra](05-purusha-vicaya.md) — on the detailed knowledge about the person (pp. 477-484)
6. [Śarīravicaya-śārīra](06-sharira-vicaya.md) — on the detailed knowledge of the body (pp. 484-492)
7. [Śarīrasaṃkhyā-śārīra](07-sharira-samkhya.md) — on the enumeration of body parts (pp. 493-499)
8. [Jātisūtrīya-śārīra](08-jatisutriya.md) — on the principle of procreation (pp. 499-526)
