द्वितीयोऽध्यायः

CHAPTER II

अथातोऽतुल्यगोत्रीयं शारीरं व्याख्यास्यामः ॥१॥

Now (I) shall expound the chapter beginning with 'dissimilar clan'. [1]

इति ह स्माह भगवानात्रेयः ॥२॥

As propounded by Lord Ātreya. [2]

[Devanagari unclear: long verse on śukra]

What is that of a man which having four constituents and six sources and
which coming out of him while cohabiting with a woman of a dissimilar clan
in a lonely place after the period of her menses is converted into embryo in the
woman. [2-3]

[Devanagari unclear: verse on śukra]

The wise call it as 'śukra' (semen) which is implanted for the origin of embryo. It
is constituted of four quarters–vāyu, agni, ap and (four mahābhūtas) and is
originated from (food consisting of) six rasas. [4]

[Devanagari unclear: verse on quality of śukra]

The wise also says, the śukra of a man is in normal condition, the man is healthy, similarly,
the woman has good śoṇita (ovum) provided that one has cohabited with a woman in
the time of her menses. [5]

[Devanagari unclear: long verses on Ātreya's reply]

How and with what factors is the foetus delivered fully developed in time and
with ease? How does even a fertile woman face delay in conception and why some-
times is the embryo destroyed? [5]

[Devanagari unclear: verses on Ātreya's reply]

By the excellence of sperm, ovum, the self, uterus and time and by management
with wholesome diet the normal foetus gets delivered with all the developed state, in time
and with ease.
