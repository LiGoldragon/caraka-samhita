निदानस्थानम्

प्रथमोऽध्यायः

CHAPTER I

अथातो ज्वरनिदानं व्याख्यास्यामः ॥१॥

Now (I) shall expound the chapter on diagnosis of fever. [1]

इति ह स्माह भगवानात्रेयः ॥२॥

As propounded by Lord Ātreya. [2]

[Devanagari unclear: verses on synonyms of nidāna]

Here hetu, nimitta, āyatana, kartṛ, kāraṇa, pratyaya and samutthāna–these are
synonyms of nidān. It is of three types–unsuitable conjunction of sense organs with
their objects, intellectual error and consequence. [3]

[Devanagari unclear: verses on three types of disease]

From these, three trpes of (somatic) diseases arise–āgneya (paittika),
saumya (kaphaja) and (vātika). The others (psychic) are of two types–rājasa and
tāmasa. [4]

[Devanagari unclear: verse on synonyms of roga]

Here vyādhi, āmaya, gada, ātaṅka, yakṣmā, jwara and vikāra–these are synonyms
of roga. [5]

[Devanagari unclear: verse on means of knowledge]

Knowledge of disease (is acquired) through nidāna (etiology), pūrvarūpa
(prodroma), liṅga (signs and symptoms), upaśaya (therapeutic suitability) and
samprāpti (pathogenesis). [6]

[Devanagari unclear: verse on nidāna]

Nidāna is the etiological factor, as already said. [7]

[Devanagari unclear: verse on pūrvarūpa]

Pūrvarūpa consists of the (unmanifested) symptoms which precede the appear-
ance of the disease. [8]
