60                            CARAKA-SAṂHITĀ                             [CH.

One should not praise and disclose self-secrets. One should always behave in
such a way that the normal period of life-span and auspicious deeds are not
lost. [23]

रहस्येष्वितरेषु वा शब्देष्वमान्दाह्वयेतु, न मत्तोन्मत्तप्रेष्ठधुष्कसम्बिहचलस्थः, मृद्दित्वचा, भूमौ संशयवा सगुरुपमावल्यप्प्त्यति-
कर्मिः, केचिचीन्मात्रालयोपाण्डीर्यचीमर्य तत्र, शरक्यंन्यां ने, ते क्रम स त्यजेत् ब्रूयात्,
पान्पमत्रा त्वजयेतेत प्रदारशरस्य पराशी हृतमर्व, शगाने षौ श्रूतू महादिःनीकुत्तगसनरिकदर्शमर्ति ॥२४॥

One should not be called from behind neither in secrets nor in public; one should
not converse with an intoxicated, insane, very aggrieved, greedy, coward, uncivil,
with fallen, sinner, dwarf, disrespected by elders, defectives in body parts, merciless,
foolish, denigrator, devoid of propriety, useless, betrayer, condemned by people,
killer of cows, killer of attacker, destroyer, seller of secrets and cheater. [24]

जानु नासाहकूरुहतस्मानहानेत न चीसात्तणायिबुहास्य प्रसीनक्त्रा वेनी निःलक्कितनानि नेषि,
न प्राथादू नाज्ञाय शुद्धसं क्षुधा क्षत्रेयश्च नारतगमिनास्य नावतिर्य्यद्गम नामृत पसोन्योभेर्वा नाज्ञानयो गान्धा,
आपगेष नयू अनंसत्य् वयतीथाधिशयेत्त् ॥२५॥

One should not cross the shadow of worshippable person; one should not go
through river without boat, should not travel by defective vehicles, should not ascend
bull with long horns, should not ride defective, unsubdued, untrained, strange, injured,
frightened, hungry, leanly, poor-limbed, ill, ill-tempered, vocal, without reins and
stop-signal animals. [25]

निर्विमार्गाणामेगेधवमायाशिश्च्चित्, दीनाज्याप्तेयेष्विहयः वमुशाज्गचेतसायेनुज्वोघि परेदग्रहेः, न क्षेमवृन्मरे षौन्मेव
न निष्क्रकर्मधो यदि कलहकलैरिति्रेतेेघु चा गेन बवेदेनम्, न तज्यगानेष्वायः न क्षर्थाणाघर्म इन्द्रानमन, ने न षत्योगीय ॥२६॥

One should not spit, sneeze, release flatus, laugh etc. in great congregation;
should not create rupture (wind), in sacrifices, should not work while concentrating
on other works, should not extract one's interest without giving due share, should not
disclose, at public, other's obligation on self, should not accept prohibited things,
should not collect them, should not cross a river by swimming, should not ride boat
in storm and turbulent water conditions, should not go or stand on the side of a falling
cloud, flame, piled etc. should not lodge oneself behind the group of cows, fire, arms
(snake) etc. [26]

नैकाधिज्ञाय नेहं नाज्ञाय्य सिंहासनाहर्वति न सिद्धासनाधिकरातरोगोवेदमसुदरणमक्षि,
इमें नाधिष्ठेय् नापपीडितो न मानम्रस्नायानम्ता तदाप्यमिनविधयथा यदि निशेख कवेत् ॥२७॥
