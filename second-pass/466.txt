चतुर्थोऽध्यायः

CHAPTER IV

अथातो महतीं गर्भावक्रान्तिं शारीरं व्याख्यास्यामः ॥१॥

Now (I) shall expound the major chapter on descent of embryo (development of
foetus). (1)

इति ह स्माह भगवानात्रेयः ॥२॥

As propounded Lord by Ātreya. (2)

[Devanagari unclear: long verse on factors of foetus]

The factors from which the embryo is originated, definition of embryo, factors
producing the embryo, the order of its development, the cause of development,
factors hindering its birth, factors leading to its destruction in the womb and
those causing morbidity though not fully destroying it–all this will be explained (here-
after). [3]

[Devanagari unclear: verse on suitability and psyche]

Embryo is originated by the aggregate of these entities–mother, father, self, suit-
ability, nutrition and psyche. The different parts being origintited from these factors
as maternal, paternal etc. have already been said earlier. [4]

[Devanagari unclear: verse on combination]

The combination of sperm, ovum and life-principle implanted in the womb is
known as embryo. [5]

[Devanagari unclear: verse on five mahābhūtas]

Embryo is the product of ākāśa, vāyu, tejas, ap and pṛthivī being the seat of con-
sciousness. Thus, embryo is the aggregate of the five mahābhūtas being the seat of
the conciousness which is regarded as the sixth constituent of embryo. [6]
