The best ones numbering one hundred fifty two, as mentioned above, are quite

[Devanagari unclear: verses on excellent ones]

The excellent ones have been mentioned among the entities having similar ac-
tions alongwith their superiority and importance in effectiveness. The entities allevi-
ating vāta, pitta and kapha and also prominent diseases have been mentioned. Having
learnt them carefully the physician should implement them in therapeutics, doing so
he be invariably attains virtue and enjoyments. [42-44]
