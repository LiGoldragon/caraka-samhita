भवन्ति चात्र–

[Devanagari unclear: verse on three psyche]

The dirty psyche gets cleaned by these means like a mirror by oil, cloth, brush
etc. then the psyche, being purified shines like the solar disc uncovered by any other
planet, clouds, dust, smoke and fog. That psyche concentrated into the self with all
docrs closed illumines like the lamp situated in the lamp case, pure, stable and with
pleasant light. [13-15]
