[Devanagari unclear: long verse on physician]

The physician, though observing the signs of death, should not disclose the
approaching death without having been requested for. Even on request, he should
not express it if is liable to cause patient's death or affliction to somebody else. The
experienced physician, without expressing patient's death should not be inclined to
treat him after observing the fatal signs. [62-64]

[Devanagari unclear: long verse on contrary signs]

In case the physician observes the signs as contrary to those of death, he should
certainly tell about the approaching condition of health as indicated by favourable
messengers, propentous signs in the way or the patient's house, the conduct and
behaviour of the patient and availability of the desired medicaments. [65-66]
