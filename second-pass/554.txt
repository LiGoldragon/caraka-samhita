If the sputum, stool and semen sink in water it indicates the end of patient's life.

If the sputum has various shades of colour and sinks in water, the patient does

When pitta following body heat reaches the temporals and causes disorder, the
disease is named as ṣaṃkhaka which kills the patient within three days.

The patient having frequent frothy haemoptysis and piercing pain in abdomen
should be discarded.

Severe loss of strength and flesh, aggravation of disease and anorexia indicate
the death of the patient within three fortnights. [18-22]

Now the summing up verses–

These signs in human beings arise at the approach of death. The physician should
observe these and other similar signs. They all are observed but are not necessarily
found in one person approaching death. Hence all the signs should be known. [23-24]
