भवति चात्र–

Here is the verse–

[Devanagari unclear: verse on dietary methodology]

One should promote the well-being of his own self by examining the eight fac-
tors of the method of dieting well. He should also adopt any other course which is
conducive to well-being. [14]

[Devanagari unclear: long verse on āmāśaya]

Where are the eatables, chewables, drinkables and lickables digested ? We sub-
mit this query to you O perseverant and enlightened one! kindly solve it. Thus interro-
gated by the disciples led by Agniveśa, Punarvasu described to them the organ where
food is digested. Between naval and breast there is an organ named 'āmāśaya' (stom-
ach), eatable, chewables, drinkables and lickables are digested there. The food having
gone to āmāśaya and having been digested there fully, its mature product thereafter
reaches all the organs through blood vessels. [15-18]

The symptoms and effects of the food taken in appropriate and inappropriate
quantity are described separately (in this chapter). [19]
