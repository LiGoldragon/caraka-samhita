[Devanagari unclear: verses on excretion mechanisms]

For the supporting materials, excretion as well as essence, rasa produced by food
acts as the means. They, according to division, fill up respective supporting
materials, thus this body is a product of the food–eaten, drunk, licked and devoured.
Similarly, the diseases also are produced by the food–eaten, drunk, licked and
devoured. Wholesome and unwholesome food produce good and bad effects respec-
tively. [5]

[Devanagari unclear: verses on Agniveśa's question]

After Lord Ātreya finished his talk, Agniveśa asked–it is observed, sir, that the
persons taking wholesome food are both diseased and healthy, similarly, in case of
the unwholesome food. In the face of such observations how the difference in good
and bad effects can be attributed to the intake of wholesome and unwholesome food
respectively? [6]

[Devanagari unclear: verses on Lord Ātreya's reply]
