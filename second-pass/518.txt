[Devanagari unclear: long verse on swelling complications]

On improper processing of the cord, there is risk of the complications such
as āyāmottundita (vertical swelling), vyāyāmottundita (horizontal swelling), piṇḍalikā
(circular hardness), vināmikā (marginal swelling) and vijṛmbhikā (umbilical hernia).
This should be treated, according to severity, with massage, annointing and bathing
with ghees which are free from burning effect and are able to alleviate vāta and
pitta. [45]

[Devanagari unclear: long verse on first feeding]

Thereafter, the birth rite of the child should be performed such as-first he should
be given to ingest the combination of honey and ghee processed with mantras as pre-
scribed in scriptures. Thereafter by the same method, the right breast should be ad-
vanced to be suckled (by the child). A water-pitcher processed with mantras should
also be kept on the side of his head. [46]

[Devanagari unclear: long verse on protection]

all over the maternity home. The oblation of offering of rice to the fire should be
