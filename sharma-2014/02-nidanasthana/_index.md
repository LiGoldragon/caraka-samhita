# Nidānasthāna — Section on Diagnosis

*Source pages 290-338 of Sharma 2014, Vol. I.*

The Nidānasthāna ("section on diagnosis") sets out the framework by which disease is to be known: the fivefold means of diagnosis (*nidāna, pūrvarūpa, liṅga, upaśaya, samprāpti*) and the detailed etiology, prodroma, symptomatology, complications and prognosis of the eight earliest somatic disorders — fever, internal haemorrhage, abdominal lump, urinary disorders, leprosy, phthisis, insanity and epilepsy. It contains 8 chapters.
