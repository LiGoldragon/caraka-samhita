# Vimānasthāna — Section of Specific Features

*Source pages 339-433 of Sharma 2014, Vol. I.*

The Vimānasthāna ("section of specific features") sets out the specific features by which the physician must read *doṣas*, drugs, place, time, strength, body, *sāra*, diet, suitability, psyche, constitution and age before treatment. It opens with the specific features of *rasa* and the eight factors of the dietary method, moves through the three-portioned belly, epidemics, the three sources of knowledge, the channels (*srotas*) and the groups of diseases, and culminates in the long eighth chapter on the examination of treatise, teacher, disciple, debate and the ten entities of therapeutic action. It contains 8 chapters.
